> ___

> - **Hit Points** 1 (1d4-1)
> - **Speed** 20 ft., climb 20 ft.
>___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|

> - **Senses** darkvision 30 ft., passive Perception 10
> - **Languages** —
> - **Challenge** 0 (10 XP)
> ___
> ***Spider Climb.*** The spider can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.
>
> ***Web Sense.*** While in contact with a web, the spider knows the exact location of any other creature in contact with the same web.
>
> ***Web Walker.*** The spider ignores movement restrictions caused by webbing.
>
> ### Actions
> ***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 1 piercing damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 9 Constitution saving throw or take 2 (1d4) poison damage.

> ## Crystal Spider
>*Medium beast, unaligned*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 14 (natural armor) 
> - **Hit Points** 13 (2d8 + 4)
> - **Speed** 30 ft., climb 30 ft.
>___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|14 (+2)|14 (+2)|14 (+2)|2 (-4)|11 (+0)|4 (-3)|
>___
> - **Skills** Stealth +4
> - **Damage Vulnerabilities** bludgeoning
> - **Senses** blindsight 10 ft., darkvision 60 ft., <br>passive Perception 10
> - **Languages** —
> - **Challenge** 1/2  (100 XP)
> ___
> ***Spider Climb.*** The spider can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.
>
> ***Web Sense.*** While in contact with a web, the spider knows the exact location of any other creature in contact with the same web.
>
> ***Web Walker.*** The spider ignores movement restrictions caused by webbing.
>
> ### Actions
> ***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC 10 Constitution saving throw, taking 4 (1d8) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. If the poison damage reduces the target to 0 hit points, the target is stable but poisoned for 1 hour, even after regaining hit points, and is paralyzed while poisoned in this way.
>
> ***Web (Recharge 6}.*** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, range 30/60 ft., one creature. *Hit:* The target is then restrained by webbing. As an action, the restrained target can make a DC 12 Strength check, bursting the webbing on a success. The webbing can also be attacked and destroyed (AC 10; hp 5; vulnerability to fire damage; immunity to bludgeoning, poison, and psychic damage).

> ##### Variant: Ice Spider
> Spiders inhabit even the coldest regions of Azeroth. An ice spider is a strange breed of spiders made of ice and crystals.
>
> An ice spider has the same statistics as a crystal spider except it is vulnerable to fire and resistant to cold damage, and deals cold damage instead of poison damage with its bite. Additionally, the spiders web deals 1 (1d6) acid damage to a creature restained by it at the beginning of the creatures turn.

> ##### Variant: Giant Deathweb Spider
> A deathweb spider has a challenge rating of 2 (450 XP). It has the same statistics as a giant spider except that it has Constitution 14, 37 (5d10 + 10) hit points, and replaces the giant spider's bite action option with the following actions. Additionally, the spiders web deals 9 (2d8) acid damage to a creature restained by it at the beginning of the creatures turn.
>
> ***Infected Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* 12 (2d8 + 3) piercing damage plus 3 (1d6) acid damage.
>
> ***Burning Acid (Recharge after a Short or Long rest).*** The spider spews forth a burning hot acid. Each creature in a 10-foot cone must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw, taking 13 (3d8) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.

> ## Shale Spider

> ___
> - **Armor Class** 15 (natural armor)
> - **Hit Points** 42 (5d10 + 15)
> - **Speed** 30 ft.

>___
> - **Damage Resistances** bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical weapons that aren't adamantine
> - **Damage Immunities** poison
> - **Condition Immunities** poisoned
> - **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 10
> - **Languages** —
> - **Challenge** 3 (700 XP)

> ***False Appearance.*** As long as the shale spider remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from a boulder or other natural rock formation.
>
> ***Spider Climb.*** The spider can climb difficult surfaces, including
upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an
ability check.
>
>
> ### Actions
>
> ***Multiattack.*** The shade spider makes two attacks: one with its bite and one slam attack.
>

>
> ***Slam.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d8 + 3) bludgeoning damage.

> ##### Variant: Lava Spider

> It has immunity only to fire, vulnerability to cold and it has the following trait in place of shale spider's False Appearance:
>
> <br>    ***Fire Form.*** A creature that touches the lava spider <br>    or hits it with a melee attack while within 5 feet of <br>    it takes 2 (1d4) fire damage and catches fire; until <br>    someone takes an action to douse the fire, the <br>    creature takes 2 (1d4) fire damage at the start of <br>    each of its turns.
>
> <br> In addition, a lava spider has the following action options in place of the shale spider's Slam:
>
> <br>    ***Multiattack.*** The shade spider makes two bite <br>    attacks: one with its bite and one with its spit fire.
>
> <br>    ***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., <br>    one target. Hit: 6 (1d8 + 2) piercing damage plus <br>    3 (1d6) fire damage.
>
> <br>    ***Spit Fire.*** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, range <br>    15/30 ft., one target.
*Hit:* 9 (2d6 + 2) fire damage.
